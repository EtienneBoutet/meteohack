Visualizing Climate Change

Climate change one of the most critical challenge faced by Canadians in the country's history. We designed a series of visualizations to help us better understand it's growing prevalence. The visualizations use data provided by Environment and Climate Change Canada. They were realized during the METEOHACK hackathon.


Visualisation of the temperature trends over a Canadian weather station. The data is arranged radially my month. The vertical axis displays temperature. As seasons go by, the average temperature goes up and down. For some stations, global warming causes the temperature to spin higher every year.
